# 🚨 Business Problem

## 📌 Organization Background

BlueCoast Retail Group is a rapidly expanding retail and e-commerce company with an increasing number of remote employees and growing online traffic.

As the business scaled, its IT infrastructure did not evolve at the same pace, leading to multiple security and operational risks.

---

## ⚠️ Key Challenges

### 1. Insecure Remote Access

Employees are accessing internal systems remotely without secure authentication mechanisms, increasing the risk of unauthorized access.

### 2. Outdated Infrastructure

Legacy systems are still in use, lacking modern security controls such as monitoring, intrusion detection, and centralized logging.

### 3. Poor Network Security

* No proper firewall configuration
* No IDS/IPS in place
* Lack of segmentation between systems

### 4. Risk of Cyber Attacks

The organization is vulnerable to:

* Brute-force attacks
* Malware infections
* Data breaches
* Denial-of-Service (DoS) attacks

### 5. Lack of Monitoring & Visibility

There is no centralized system to:

* Monitor logs
* Detect threats in real time
* Respond to incidents

---

## 💥 Business Impact

If not addressed, these issues can lead to:

* Financial losses
* Customer data compromise
* Reputation damage
* Service downtime

---

## ✅ Conclusion

BlueCoast Retail Group requires a complete overhaul of its infrastructure to implement secure access, monitoring, and protection mechanisms aligned with modern cybersecurity practices.
